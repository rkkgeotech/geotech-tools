Parameter Identification Sequence (Sec. 3.1.3)
  1. Identify φ' and c' (stress paths from CICD tests at three σ₃ values)
  2. Identify ψ (from ε_v − ε₁ curve slope at failure)
  3. Identify R_f and E₅₀ at each confining pressure σ₃
  4. Identify E_ur at each σ₃ (assign ν_ur ≈ 0.15–0.20)
  5. Fit power-law for m using ln(E₅₀) vs ln[(σ₃+c·cotφ)/(σ_ref+c·cotφ)]
  6. Choose σ_ref ≈ in-situ confining stress; evaluate E₀_ref, E_ur_ref, E₅₀_ref
  7. Double-check: E₀ > E_ur > E₅₀  ·  E_ur/E₅₀ > 2  ·  3.6 < E₀/E₅₀ < 30
  8. Evaluate K₀ᴺᶜ and E_oed_ref at σ_ref_oed (primary loading curve) to compute M and H
  9. Determine OCR profile (constant OCR or q_POP for variable profile)
  10. Set KSR₀ = K₀ᴺᶜ for natural K₀-consolidated soils; compute K₀ with Eq. 2.27
