Calculator Inputs
Calculation Mode
Mode A: Select a recommended (nominal) pile section and corrosion loss. The calculator applies loss to all exposed surfaces and reports the reduced Ix and Sx, plus the closest standard section whose tabulated properties fit within the corroded capacity.
Mode B: Specify the required design properties (Sx and Ix your structural analysis demands after corrosion). The calculator returns all nominal pile sections that, when corroded, still meet or exceed those requirements — sorted by closest match.
Corrosion applied (2 × CL per dimension): dc = d − 2·CL bf,c = bf − 2·CL tf,c = tf − 2·CL tw,c = tw − 2·CL
